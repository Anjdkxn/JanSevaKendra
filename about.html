About Ankush Jan Seva Kendra

Ankush Jan Seva Kendra is a trusted citizen service center dedicated to providing fast, transparent, and reliable government and digital services to the public.

Who We Are

We are a professional Jan Seva Kendra offering essential services such as Aadhaar assistance, banking services, pension schemes, insurance services, and online applications under one roof.

What We Do

Our center bridges the gap between citizens and digital government services by simplifying online processes and ensuring quick and accurate service delivery.

Why Choose Us

We focus on transparency, customer satisfaction, affordable pricing, and timely service with complete guidance for every application.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower citizens by making government and digital services accessible, efficient, and hassle-free for everyone, especially in rural and semi-urban areas.
